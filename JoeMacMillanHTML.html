Joe MacMillan

The central genius behind TV series Halt and Catch Fire

Descriptions of the actions of Joe MacMillan, according to each season of the series:

  • Season One
    Insinuates himself into the company Cardiff Electric. Teams up with Cameron, Gordon and Boz to reverse engineer a competitor to the dominant IBM computer. Destroys the first shipment of the Giant.
  • Season Two
    Breaks up with Cameron, and dates a journalist with a wealthy father. Attempts to re-invent himself out west in California.
  • Season Three
    Becomes a Steve Jobs-like figure and is further alienated from those he connected with at Cardiff Electric.
  • Season Four
    Has a reckoning of personality, and finds his way back to Houston to help create a search engine with Cameron, Donna and Gord. Leaves, and becomes a teacher for some reason.

"The computer isn't the thing. It's the thing that gets you to the thing."

-- Joe MacMillan
